What Happens When You Pass By a Beacon?

A notification will pop up with a very simple message (Example: Upcoming Volleyball game). Simply click on this notification to get more detailed info about this event.

Can The App Be Used Without Being Near a Beacon?

Simple Answer: YES

This is how it can be done...

  1. Login using your username and password.
  2. Click on "Events" to see a list of all events that are coming up on campus.
  3. Click on "Building Info" to see a list of all the buildings on campus and information about them.
  4. Click on "Settings" to toggle which notifications you wish to see and not see when you pass a beacon.
  5. Click on "Help" to find detailed info on how to use the app or contact us for help.
  6. REMEMBER: only those with admin accounts can create, edit, and delete events.
